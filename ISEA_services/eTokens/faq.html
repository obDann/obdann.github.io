Who do I contact about eToken Related issues or inquiries?

All departments across all three campuses have Departmental eToken Adminsitrators who are responsible for the administration of tokens in the department.

  • If you have an eToken and are unsure of who your admin is please contact the individual who gave you the eToke or access the eToken Departmental Administrator list.
  • If you do not have an eToken but need to obtain one please contact your business officer or the department representative of the system which you attempting to access (ROSI,AMS,STAREZ,ETC)
If you are still experiencing difficulties please contact auth.admin@utoronto.ca

Who is my Department Administrator?

All departments across all three campuses have Departmental eToken Adminsitrators who are responsible for the administration of tokens in the department.

You can access a list of Administrators by accessing our Departmental Administrator list.

How do I change my eToken Password?

  1. Open up Safenet Authentication Client Tools (if you are unable to locate the program open the start menu and preform an application search by typing in the program name)
  2. Select ‘Change token password’
  3. Enter your eToken’s current password in the requested field
  4. Enter your new token Password in both requested fields
  5. Select 'Ok'
